How it works

From application to term sheet in 5 minutes

No paperwork, no waiting. Our AI handles the feasibility analysis that usually takes days.

Drop in a valuation, financial statements, QS report, or loan summary. Our AI reads your docs and pre-fills your entire Funding Request automatically. No documents? No problem — answer a few guided questions instead.
